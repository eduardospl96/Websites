CAMPING KIT

COOKING

Tableware Sets

For each camper, a bowl, mug, and utensil set from W&P's Porter Collection and linen napkins. The bowl and mug latch shut to store and protect your food from pests. The utensil set includes a stainless steel knife, fork, and spoon in a silicon carrying case.

Cooking

The reservation includes a stove, tank of propane, and plenty of food preparation space. The provided cookware set includes a skillet, pot, and kettle.

Food Preparation

All of the essential utensils for cooking and serving, including a cutting board and kitchen towels.

Food Storage

Each campsite is supplied with a latching cooler with a motion-sensor light inside.

SLEEPING

The Tent

Each guest is accommodated in a Woods A-Frame which includes a rain fly that protects against wet weather and can be removed for a view of the sky.

Double Sleeping Bag

Campers have the option of cuddling up together in a Farenheight Mammoth double-sleeping bag by Teton Sports. The oversized and plush sleeping bag is rated for temperatures as low as 20 ºF and is paired with a a liner sheet which also works as a lighter cover for warm nights.

Single Sleeping Bags

Campers who prefer to sleep separately will have the Farenheight XXL single sleeping bag by Teton Sports. The oversized and plush sleeping bag is rated for temperatures as low as 20 ºF and is paired with a a liner sheet which also works as a lighter cover for warm nights.

The Bed

For each camper, a pillow and ComfortLite XXL sleeping pad by Teton Sports are provided. Two sleeping pads may be attached for use with the double sleeping bag, or separated for us with the single sleeping bags.

Tent Lamp

Convenient for rummanging or reading, hanging inside each tent is a Beacon Hanging Lantern Light.

LOUNGING

Seating

Each camper in the reservation will have a study and snug CLIQ Chair, the famously "bottle-sized chair for anywhere".

Lantern

For each campsite a Railroad Lantern by Barebones Living which casts a warm incandescent light.

CLEANING & SAFETY

Washing Up

Each reservation includes castlle soap and an Ayate Washcloth from Public Goods and a Dishy Dish Rack by Rig-Tig, a compact 2-in-1 wash basin and drying rack.

Rubbish

Always remember to leave no trace. The provided Sea-to-Summit 10 liter trash dry sack comes with compostable trash bags for packing out waste.

First Aid Kit

This first aid kit contains all the essentials for treating minor cuts and scrapes.
