CAMPING KIT

COOKING

Cooking

The reservation includes a grill table with a two-burner stove, tank of propane, and plenty of food preparation space.

Food Preparation

The provided cookware set includes a skillet, pot, and kettle. All of the essential utensils for cooking and serving, including a cutting board and kitchen towels, are stored in the grill table.

Food Storage

Each campsite is supplied with a latching cooler with a motion-sensor light inside.

Tableware Sets

For each camper, a a bowl, mug, and utensil set from W&P's Porter Collection. The bowl and mug are made of silcone-wrapped ceramic and include latching to store and protect your food from pests. The utensil set includes a stainless steel knife, fork, and spoon in a silicon carrying case.

SLEEPING

The Tent

Each guest is accommodated in a Woods A-Frame which includes a rain fly that protects against wet weather and can be removed for a view of the sky.

Double Sleeping Bag

Campers have the option of cuddling up together in a Farenheight Mammoth double-sleeping bag by Teton Sports. The oversized and plush sleeping bag is rated for temperatures as low as 20f degrees and is paired with a a liner sheet which also works as a lighter cover for warm nights.

Single Sleeping Bags

Campers who prefer to sleep separately will have the Farenheight XXL single sleeping bag by Teton Sports. The oversized and plush sleeping bag is rated for temperatures as low as 20f degrees and is paired with a a liner sheet which also works as a lighter cover for warm nights.

The Bed

For each camper, a pillow and ComfortLite XXL sleeping pad by Teton Sports are provided. Two sleeping pads may be attached for use with the double sleeping bag, or separated for us with the single sleeping bags.

Tent Lamp

Convenient for rummanging or reading, hanging inside each tent is a Beacon Hanging Lantern Light.

LOUNGING

Seating

For each guest, an oversized Woods Mammoth Folding Padded Chair to get cozy in and a blanket for keeping warm in front of the fire.

Shade

An adjustable umbrella and side table create a cool and shaded sitting area.

Lantern

For each campsite a Railroad Lantern by Barebones Living which casts a warm incandescent light.

Power

Included is a charged power bank for topping up the lanterns and any of the campers guests.

CLEANING & SAFETY

Washing Up

Each reservation includes a dish basin with castile soap for washing hands and dishes, a sponge, and wash cloth.

Rubbish

The provided dry sack with composable trash bags gives your guests a place to store their waste during their stay.

First Aid Kit

This first aid kit contains all the essentials for treating minor cuts and scrapes.
